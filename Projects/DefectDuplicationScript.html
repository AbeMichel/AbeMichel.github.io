Rally to Jira Defect Duplication

---

While at Deere our team communicated findings through both Jira and Rally which led to quite a bit of duplicated work. There were errors/discrepencies that went unnoticed when manually entering information into both. To combat this I worked with the Rally and Jira APIs to pull entries from Rally then reformat and publish them to Jira. While being a small prject, the goal was to save time and improve our communication with other teams and that was accomplished.
