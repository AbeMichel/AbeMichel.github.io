Wind-Up Car

Lessons Learned

  1. Communicate early and often

---

After a semester of engineering classes we were tasked with designing and assembling a project utilizing hydraulics or gear ratios. My group decided on a wind-up car utilizing gear ratios. I took lead of the modeling in Solidworks preparing the gears and frame for 3D printing and CNC respectively. While the project itself was simple I was beginning to learn about the engineering process and what designing on a team meant.

There were troubles when it came to scheduling for our team working around various sports. A majority of our issues could have been avoided had we communicated earlier and more often.
